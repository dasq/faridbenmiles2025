About

Farid

[EN] Farid Ben Miles is a captivating fingerstyle guitarist whose music embodies his rich cultural heritage and extensive global experiences. Raised in the suburbs of Paris by Algerian parents, Farid's upbringing was infused with the vibrant rhythms of North Africa and the enchanting melodies of the Mediterranean.

Having pursued an engineering degree where he explored the nuances of the electric guitar, Farid embarked on a transformative journey of self-discovery, traversing continents with just a backpack and his guitar. Today, Farid's music transcends boundaries, weaving narratives of love, loss, and longing, inviting listeners on an emotive journey through uncharted territories.

Inspired by musical luminaries such as Andy McKee, John Butler, and Rodrigo y Gabriela, Farid has cultivated a distinctive style blending elements of Flamenco and Blues with influences from Arab and European traditions.

As a performer, Farid captivates audiences with his soul-stirring melodies and intricate fingerstyle technique, transporting them to distant realms with each note. With a diverse repertoire spanning continents and genres, Farid Ben Miles is a versatile artist whose music celebrates cultural diversity and fosters unity among audiences worldwide.

Concerts

My music is the result of the fundamental lessons drawn from my life which I share with jubilation. Since my first performance at a little contest won in Dresden, I’ve had the chance to appear in very different situations: from living room concerts to festivals with over 500 people and from 10 minutes to 4 hours concerts.
The simple use of an acoustic guitar, the virtuosic act with this same instrument, the tale of the stories behind each song, the fusion of musical genres, and the wide repertoire mixing compositions and covers are, I believe, a part of what allowed me to adapt myself in many live scenarios. You will find here a non-exhaustive list but representative of the panel of performances I have been able to realize.
